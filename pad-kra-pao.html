Pad Kra Pao

Level Of Difficulties: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

Ingredients

  • 800g minced pork
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 shallot
  • half a thai red pepper
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 4 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tbs oyster sauce
  • 1 tbs fish sauce
  • basil leaves - as many as you would like
  • 3 eggs

Instructions

  • Fry up some sunny side up eggs and set them aside.
  • Mince garlic, shallots, and pepper, then sauté them in a pan.
  • Turn the heat to high, add in the minced pork, break it into small bits, and let it crisp up.
  • While the pork is cooking, mix up the sauce using sugar, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce.
  • Once the pork turns a honey-like color, pour in the sauce.
  • When the meat is golden-brown and crispy—meaning all the moisture has cooked off—toss in the basil leaves.
  • Serve hot with rice and the sunny side up eggs on top.
