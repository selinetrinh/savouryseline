Matcha Oatmilk Latte

Level Of Difficulties: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆

Ingredients

  • 5g matcha ceremonial grade powder
  • 60ml 80°C water
  • 200ml oatmilk
  • lots of ice cubes

Instructions

  • Soak your chasen (bamboo whisk) in hot water for about 1 minute to soften it up.
  • Sift your matcha powder (I love using Wako from Marukyu Koyamaen) into a bowl.
  • Add about 20ml of 80°C water to the matcha and whisk in a circular motion to smooth it out.
  • Pour in the rest of the water and whisk vigorously in a W-motion for about 30 seconds.
  • Lift your hand slightly and continue whisking in a W-motion until you get a nice layer of microfoam on top.
  • Gently pop any large bubbles on the surface to keep it smooth.
  • Fill a cup with ice and oat milk (I usually go with Oatly or Elmhurst), then slowly pour the matcha over.
  • This is ideally how I start my day!
