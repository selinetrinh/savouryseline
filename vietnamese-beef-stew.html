Vietnamese Beef Stew

Level Of Difficulties: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

Ingredients

  • 500g beef brisket
  • 50ml red wine
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 70g carrots
  • 10g sweet onion
  • 10g shallots
  • 10g garlic
  • 10g ginger
  • 1 anise star
  • 5g cinnamon
  • 10ml fish sauce
  • 2 tsp chicken powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 20g tapioca starch

Instructions

  • Cut the beef brisket into 1x1 inch cubes.
  • Season the beef with sweet onion, shallots, garlic, ginger, anise star, cinnamon, fish sauce, chicken powder, chili powder, black pepper, and a splash of red wine.
  • Sauté everything until the beef is nicely browned on the outside (like a rich, dark sear).
  • Blend tomatoes into a liquid and pour that into the pot with the beef. Add about 1 liter of water.
  • Chop up carrots and potatoes, toss them into the pot, and cook until they're tender.
  • Mix some tapioca starch with 20ml of water, then stir it into the pot to give the broth that silky texture.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with some crusty bread.
  • Bon appétit!
