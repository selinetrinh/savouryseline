Sesame Latte

Level Of Difficulties: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆

Ingredients

  • 120ml espresso/li>
  • 50ml whipping cream
  • 1 tbs condensed milk
  • 15g sesame powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • 100ml milk

Instructions

  • Whip heavy cream with sugar, a pinch of salt, condensed milk, and sesame powder until it becomes thick and silky.
  • Pour milk into a cup as the base layer.
  • Spoon the whipped cream mixture gently over the milk.
  • Top it off with a shot of your favorite espresso.
  • Enjoy your morning boost!
